# Product Context: Bihar Local Street Food Guide

This project understands local food culture of Bihar, India.

## Region
- Bihar (Patna, Motihari, Muzaffarpur)

## Popular Street Foods
- Litti Chokha: Roasted wheat balls stuffed with sattu, served with mashed vegetables
- Chana Ghugni: Spicy boiled chickpeas eaten in the evening
- Thekua: Traditional sweet made during Chhath Puja
- Khaja: Crispy layered sweet from Silao
- Sattu Sharbat: Cooling summer drink made from roasted gram flour

## Popular Locations
- Patna Junction
- Gandhi Maidan
- Motihari local markets
- Bus stands and railway stations
